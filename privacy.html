Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Elder Sentry is designed with privacy at its core. We understand that the messages you check may contain sensitive information, and we take our responsibility to protect your privacy seriously.

Information We Process

Message Analysis

When you use Elder Sentry to analyze a text message or email:

  • The message content is processed by our AI system to detect potential scams
  • Analysis happens in real-time and the message is not permanently stored
  • We do not access, store, or share any personal information contained in the messages
  • Your contacts, phone numbers, and email addresses are never collected or stored

Usage Data

To improve our service and ensure it works properly, we may collect limited, anonymous usage information:

  • Number of scans performed (not the content of messages)
  • General scam type categories detected
  • App performance and crash data
  • Device type and iOS version (for compatibility purposes)

What We Do NOT Collect

Elder Sentry does not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your contacts or address book
  • Your text message or email history
  • Personal identifying information
  • Financial information
  • Location data
  • Any data from messages you don't explicitly share with the app

How We Use Information

The limited information we process is used exclusively to:

  • Analyze messages for scam indicators when you request it
  • Provide you with scam detection results and explanations
  • Improve our AI detection capabilities (using anonymous, aggregated data only)
  • Ensure the app functions properly and fix technical issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Period.

The only exception would be if required by law (such as a valid subpoena or court order), and we would notify you if legally permitted to do so.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using modern cryptographic protocols
  • Our servers and infrastructure follow security best practices
  • Access to systems is strictly limited and monitored
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use Elder Sentry without creating an account or providing personal information
  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Request information about what data we have (which is minimal)
  • Request deletion of any data we may have

Children's Privacy

Elder Sentry is designed for adults and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
