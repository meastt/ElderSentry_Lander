1. Introduction

ElderSentry ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your loved ones. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our senior safety monitoring service.

By using ElderSentry, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use our service.

Our Privacy Promise

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Your family's safety data is encrypted, secure, and used solely to provide you with the best possible monitoring and care services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and contact information
  • Emergency contact details and family member information
  • Health information relevant to monitoring (medication schedules, medical conditions)
  • Account credentials and preferences
  • Payment and billing information

Monitoring Data

To provide our safety monitoring services, we collect:

  • Movement and activity patterns
  • Environmental sensor data (temperature, lighting, etc.)
  • Device usage and connectivity information
  • Alert and notification data
  • Health metrics and wellness indicators

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Device information (type, model, operating system)
  • IP address and location data (with consent)
  • App usage statistics and performance data
  • Log files and error reports

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

Provide and Improve Our Services

  • Monitor senior safety and wellness
  • Send alerts and notifications to family members
  • Analyze patterns to detect potential issues
  • Provide health insights and recommendations
  • Improve our AI algorithms and monitoring accuracy

Communication and Support

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Conduct surveys and gather feedback
  • Provide emergency assistance when needed

Security and Compliance

  • Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations
  • Maintain system security and integrity
  • Investigate and prevent potential safety issues

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

With Your Consent

We may share information with family members, caregivers, or healthcare providers when you explicitly authorize us to do so.

Emergency Situations

In case of emergency or when we believe someone's safety is at risk, we may share relevant information with emergency services, family members, or healthcare providers.

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our service, such as:

  • Cloud storage and hosting providers
  • Payment processing services
  • Communication and notification services
  • Data analytics and security services

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users.

No Third-Party Marketing

We will never share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your privacy and trust are paramount to our mission.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Secure communication protocols (TLS 1.3)

Access Controls

  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Role-based access controls and permissions
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee training on data protection practices

Infrastructure Security

  • Secure cloud infrastructure with SOC 2 compliance
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Intrusion detection and monitoring systems
  • Backup and disaster recovery procedures

Continuous Monitoring

Our security team continuously monitors our systems 24/7 to detect and respond to any potential security threats or breaches.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • View what information we have about you

Correction and Updates

  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Update your personal details and preferences
  • Modify monitoring settings and alerts

Deletion and Restriction

  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restrict certain types of data processing
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Consent Management

  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Change privacy preferences at any time
  • Control who can access your monitoring data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

Active Accounts

For active accounts, we retain your information for the duration of your subscription and for a reasonable period afterward to provide continued support.

Monitoring Data

  • Real-time monitoring data: Stored for immediate analysis and alerts
  • Historical patterns: Retained for up to 2 years for trend analysis
  • Health insights: Stored for up to 5 years for long-term health tracking

Account Deletion

When you delete your account, we will:

  • Remove your personal information within 30 days
  • Retain anonymized data for service improvement (no personal identifiers)
  • Keep certain records as required by law (up to 7 years)

8. Children's Privacy

ElderSentry is designed for monitoring senior adults and is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove such information from our systems.

For users between 13 and 18 years of age, we require parental consent before collecting or processing any personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you of material changes via email or app notification
  • Provide a summary of key changes when significant updates are made

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Your Continued Use

Your continued use of ElderSentry after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes acceptance of those changes.
