1. What Information We Collect

To provide fall detection and safety monitoring, we need to collect certain information. Here's exactly what we gather and why:

Account Information

  • Contact details: Name, email, phone number for you and emergency contacts
  • Relationship info: Who's the senior, who are the family members/caregivers
  • Device setup: Device ID, settings, and preferences

Safety & Health Data

  • Fall detection: Movement patterns from the wearable device to detect falls
  • Activity tracking: Daily routines (wake time, meals, sleep) to spot unusual patterns
  • Medication schedules: Reminder times and confirmation when doses are taken
  • Location data: GPS coordinates during alerts (not continuous tracking)
  • Alert history: When alerts were sent and how they were resolved

Technical Information

  • Device info: Battery level, connectivity status, software version
  • App usage: Features used, settings changed (to improve our service)
  • Error logs: If something crashes, we get an anonymous error report

What we DON'T collect: We don't record audio or video. We don't track your location unless there's an alert. We don't scan your messages or contacts.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for these specific purposes:

Provide Safety Monitoring

  • Detect falls and send immediate alerts to your family
  • Track daily activities and notify you of unusual patterns
  • Send medication reminders at scheduled times
  • Provide location information during emergencies

Improve Our Service

  • Analyze (anonymized) data to improve fall detection accuracy
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Understand which features families find most helpful
  • Test new safety features before launch

Communicate With You

  • Send emergency alerts and safety notifications
  • Provide customer support when you need help
  • Send important updates about your service (we keep these rare)
  • Ask for feedback to make ElderSentry better

Marketing: We'll only send you promotional emails if you opt in. You can unsubscribe anytime with one click.

3. Who We Share With (Spoiler: Almost Nobody)

Your family's privacy is sacred to us. We share your information only in these limited situations:

People You Authorize

When you add family members or caregivers to your ElderSentry account, they can see the senior's activity data, receive alerts, and communicate through the device. You control who has access and can remove anyone anytime.

Emergency Services (When You Need Them)

If someone falls and activates emergency features, we may share their location and basic information with 911 or emergency responders. This only happens when you've configured emergency calling features.

Essential Service Providers

We work with carefully vetted partners who help us run ElderSentry:

  • Cloud hosting: Secure servers to store encrypted data (AWS or similar)
  • Communication services: SMS and push notification delivery
  • Payment processing: Handling purchases (they never see health data)

These partners are contractually required to protect your data and can only use it to provide services to us.

Legal Requirements

We'll disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect someone's safety. This is extremely rare and we'll notify you when legally permitted.

4. How We Keep Your Data Safe

We use industry-leading security practices to protect your family's information:

Encryption Everywhere

  • In transit: All data moving between your device and our servers uses bank-level encryption (TLS 1.3)
  • At rest: Everything stored in the cloud is encrypted with AES-256 encryption
  • On your device: Local data is encrypted with your device's security features

Access Controls

  • Multi-factor authentication for family accounts
  • Only authorized ElderSentry employees can access systems (and all access is logged)
  • Regular security audits by third-party experts
  • Background checks for all team members who handle data

Infrastructure Security

  • Servers hosted in SOC 2 compliant data centers
  • 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Automated backups with encrypted storage

If there's ever a security incident: We'll notify you within 72 hours via email and app notification, explain what happened, and detail the steps we're taking to protect you.

5. Your Rights and Controls

This is YOUR data. You have complete control over it:

Access Your Data

Request a copy of all information we have about you. We'll send it in a readable format (PDF or CSV) within 30 days. Just email privacy@eldersentry.com.

Correct Information

Found a mistake? You can update most information directly in the app. For other changes, contact support and we'll fix it immediately.

Delete Your Data

Want to delete your account and all associated data? We'll remove everything within 30 days of your request. Note: We may keep anonymized data for service improvement and some records as required by law (like payment records for tax purposes).

Export Your Data

Moving to a different service? We'll provide your data in a portable format so you can take it with you. No lock-in, no hassle.

Control Communications

  • Turn off marketing emails (emergency alerts always stay on for safety)
  • Choose which family members receive which types of alerts
  • Set quiet hours for non-urgent notifications
  • Customize notification preferences for each family member

For California Residents (CCPA)

If you're in California, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Contact us to exercise these rights‚Äîno verification hoops, just a simple email.

For European Residents (GDPR)

We comply with GDPR requirements including the right to data portability, right to be forgotten, and right to object to processing. Contact our privacy team for assistance.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Active account data: As long as your account is active
  • Historical alerts: 2 years (to help identify long-term patterns)
  • Activity trends: 5 years (for health tracking insights)
  • After account deletion: Most data deleted within 30 days; some records kept for legal compliance (up to 7 years)
  • Anonymized analytics: Kept indefinitely to improve fall detection (no personal identifiers)

7. Children's Privacy

ElderSentry is designed for monitoring senior adults. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you're under 18 and want to use our family app, we need parental consent.

If we discover we've accidentally collected data from a child under 13, we'll delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as we add features or comply with new regulations. When we make changes:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • For minor changes, we'll post the new policy here
  • For major changes, we'll email you and notify you in the app
  • You can review past versions by contacting us

Continuing to use ElderSentry after changes means you accept the updated policy. If you don't agree, you can delete your account anytime.
