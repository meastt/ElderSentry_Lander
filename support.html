Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up ElderSentry for my loved one?
Setting up ElderSentry is simple and takes less than 15 minutes. Download our mobile app, create an account, and follow the guided setup process. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist with installation and configuration.
What devices are compatible with ElderSentry?
ElderSentry works with smartphones, tablets, smart watches, and our dedicated monitoring devices. We support iOS 12+, Android 8+, and provide hardware options for homes without smart devices.
How does the AI monitoring work?
Our AI analyzes daily patterns, movement, medication schedules, and environmental factors to detect anomalies. It learns your loved one's normal routine and alerts you to any concerning changes or potential emergencies.
Is my family's data secure and private?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure cloud storage, and follow strict privacy protocols. Your data is never shared with third parties, and you have full control over what information is collected and stored.
What if there's a false alarm?
Our AI is designed to minimize false alarms through advanced pattern recognition. If an alert is triggered, you'll receive immediate notification with context, and you can quickly verify the situation through our app's live monitoring features.
Can multiple family members access the system?
Yes! ElderSentry supports multiple family members and caregivers. You can invite family members, set different permission levels, and ensure everyone stays informed about your loved one's wellbeing.
