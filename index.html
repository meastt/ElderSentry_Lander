Frequently Asked Questions

How does Elder Sentry work?

Simply tap the share button on any suspicious text or email, select Elder Sentry, and get an instant analysis. Our AI examines the message and tells you if it's safe or potentially a scam.

Is my data private?

Yes. We analyze messages only to detect scams. We don't store your personal information or share your data with anyone.

How much does it cost?

Elder Sentry offers free daily scans. Premium plans with unlimited checks are available for those who need them.

What types of scams can Elder Sentry detect?

Elder Sentry detects phishing emails, fake bank alerts, lottery scams, tech support fraud, imposter scams, and many other common threats targeting seniors.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use it?

Not at all. Elder Sentry was designed to be simple - just one tap and you get a clear answer in plain language.

Can I use it for both texts and emails?

Yes! Elder Sentry works with both text messages and emails on your iPhone.
