Everything You Need for Peace of Mind

Simple, effective features designed specifically for seniors and their families

🏥

Automatic Fall Detection

Advanced sensors detect falls and immediately alert your emergency contacts via SMS and phone call. No button press needed—help is on the way automatically.

📱

Real-Time Family Dashboard

See daily activity summaries, medication adherence, and location updates. Everyone in the family stays connected through our simple mobile app.

⏰

Smart Medication Tracking

Gentle reminders for medication times. Track when doses are taken and get alerts if a dose is missed. Share logs with doctors at appointments.

🔋

7-Day Battery Life

Lightweight wearable device that charges weekly. Low battery alerts ensure it's always ready. Water-resistant for shower safety.

🏠

Activity Monitoring

Track daily routines like waking up, meals, and bedtime. Unusual patterns trigger wellness check-in alerts to family members.

💬

Two-Way Voice Communication

Built-in speaker and microphone let your loved one talk directly to emergency contacts after an alert. Quick reassurance or faster help coordination.
